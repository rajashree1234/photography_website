    photography tips

  • avoid camera shake

    Camera shake or blur is something that can plague any photographer .First, you need to learn how to hold your camera correctly use both hands.

  • use simple backgrounds

    If possible, choose a plain background.

  • use a polarizing filter

    If you can buy filter for your lens,make it a polarizer.The recommended type of polarizer is circular cause these allow your camera to use TTL.

    • some other tips...


    1.experiment with shutter speed

    2.pan to create motion

    3.choose the right ISO

    4.don't use flash indoors

    5.create a sense of depth

    6.learn to use the exposure triangle

    FAQs...

    Q1.Do you deliver every image you shoot?

    Yes , we do .Once the processing gets over our members will reach out to you at your address.

    Q2.Does your studio provide videography services?

    No, sorry this facility is not available but soon it will be functional .Once we start we will definitely share it.

    Q3.What is your photography style?

    We deal with every kind of photography be it wedding,pre-wedding photoshoots ,college workshops travel photos etc.

    We shoot most of our images in Small Raw with certain situations where we move to Large Raw.

    Post production for engagement sessions are completed 6-8 weeks after the date of shoot,if before a rush process fee is charged.
